# Release Boundary

Public release follows the policy stated in the paper's Ethical Considerations section:

- Public: code, configuration files, benign/query metadata, attack-mode labels, aggregate metrics, model outputs, judge labels, and evaluation scripts.
- Controlled-access only: fuller per-case bundles containing synthetic attack documents or injected-result contexts, available upon request to bona fide research groups under no-deployment and no-live-SEO terms.
- Not released: raw copyrighted search-cache contents, live-domain infrastructure, executable payloads, and operational SEO instructions such as backlink-network construction or cloaking deployment.

This repository is therefore a public artifact package, not the complete internal experiment workspace.
The public software artifact is released under the MIT License in `LICENSE`. The license applies to the included code, configuration, documentation, aggregate metrics, judge labels, and sanitized model-output files in this public package. It does not grant rights to raw copyrighted web/search contents or controlled synthetic attack bundles, which are not included here.
